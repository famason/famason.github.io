About Us

and our goal

We care about our oceans

The preservation of oceans is extremely important for the benefit of people and the entire planet. The goal is to create an interest for people regarding aspects of the oceans in order to promote the importance of them. The oceans are fascinating, and the Pacific Ocean is the largest of them all, which we believe is the perfect place to help people build an interest in learning more about its ecosystems. Help us make the world a better place by learning more about the Pacific Ocean.
